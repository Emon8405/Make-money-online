How to Start a Blog in 2026

A simple guide for beginners.

Step 1: Choose Your Topic

Select a topic you enjoy and can consistently write about.

Step 2: Create Your Website

You can use GitHub Pages, WordPress, or another platform depending on your needs.

Step 3: Publish Helpful Content

Focus on original, useful articles that answer readers' questions.

Step 4: Learn Basic SEO

Use clear titles, headings, and internal links to help search engines understand your content.
